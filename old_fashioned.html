Bulleit Old Fashioned

Some people say that the Old Fashioned is one of the first cocktails ever created. It is light, refreshing tasting, yet complex combination of Bulleit Bourbon, sugar and bitters

    Ingredients:
  • 1.25 OZ Bulleit Bourbon
  • 2 bar spoons Simple Syrup
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 orange peel
      • Equipment:
  • Rocks glass
  • 1 jigger
  • Ice
  • Knife
  • Bar spoon
      • How to Make:
  • Add two bar spoons of simple syrup, three dashes of bitters and Bulleit Bourbon to a large rocks glass.
  • Add ice
  • Stir gently until the level of the ice and liquid equalize.
  • Zest an orange peel over hte glass then add the peel to the drink as garnish.
